---
description: Record an architecture/design decision as an ADR in docs/decisions/.
---

Create a new ADR in `docs/decisions/` numbered sequentially (`NNN-<slug>.md`).

Find the highest existing number first (`ls docs/decisions/`), then increment.

Template:

```
# NNN. <Title>

- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded by ADR-XXX

## Context
<the forces at play, why this decision is needed>

## Decision
<the decision in one or two sentences>

## Consequences
<positive, negative, and neutral consequences>

## Alternatives considered
<what else was on the table and why it lost>
```

Topic: $ARGUMENTS
